• Task 1 : Get To Sector D74
    • Step 1 : Move towards sector D74. The exact location is displayed in the station tab.
    • Step 2 : Join your teammates at checkpoint B\12
    • Step 3 : Move together towards sector D74
  • Task 2 : Device Replacement
    • Step 1 : Remove the damaged device. Turn off the power supply and use the equipment T\5 to safetely remove the B31 device.
    • Step 2 : Mount the device. Use connectors B51 e B65 to link the new device. Connect the power supply
    • Step 3 : Configuration. Start the configuration procedure of the device. Wait for further details and parameters
  • Task 3 : Test the Device
    • Step 1 : Get Status. Verify that the new device is properly configured by getting its status report
    • Step 2 : Earth Validation. Wait for the result of the analisys performed by Houston Earth Station
    • Step 3 : Complete the test. Set the flags to active mode and prepare yourself to leave the area
  • Task 4 : Return to Station
    • Step 1 : Check your Status. If you have some anomalies, use the emergency entrance E\21
    • Step 2 : Proceed towards checkpoint B\12
    • Step 3 : End of Mission.Open the main lift gate
